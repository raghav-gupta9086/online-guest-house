Simple Room Details

The Simple Room is perfect for budget travelers. It includes a single bed, clean bathroom, and basic furniture. Suitable for solo guests or short stays. Includes fan, attached bathroom, and housekeeping.

Executive Room Details

The Executive Room offers a comfortable queen-sized bed, air conditioning, LCD TV, working desk, and premium bathroom. Ideal for business travelers and couples.

Deluxe Suite Details

The Deluxe Suite is a luxury stay option featuring a king-sized bed, sofa set, balcony view, mini-fridge, wardrobe, and 24/7 service. Perfect for long stays or families.
